Romeo and Juliet

A Poem

In fair Verona where we lay our scene,
Two star-crossed lovers, caught in fate's keen.
Their hearts entwined, a bond so deep,
Amidst the feuds, their love they keep.

Through whispered vows in the moonlit night,
They dared to dream, to seek the light.
But shadows loomed, and tragedy came,
Love's gentle spark became a flame.

A secret kiss, a potion’s brew,
Two worlds apart, yet love still grew.
With every heartbeat, their fate entwined,
In death, their love, forever enshrined.

So here’s to love, in darkness and light,
A tale of passion that burns so bright.
For even in sorrow, love's flame will endure,
In hearts of the faithful, forever pure.
